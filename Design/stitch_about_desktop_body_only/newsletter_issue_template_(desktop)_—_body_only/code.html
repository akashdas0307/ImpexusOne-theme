trending_up Market Updates

Recent shifts in the geopolitical landscape have triggered a wave of new trade agreements. While North American corridors stabilize, we are seeing increased volatility in Southeast Asian manufacturing hubs.

For our clients in the automotive sector, this means a potential delay in semiconductor procurement. Conversely, textiles and raw materials are seeing a surplus, driving costs down temporarily. Here is what you need to know about the latest policy changes affecting import duties:

  • Tariff Adjustments: New 5% levy on aluminum imports from non-partner nations starting November 1st.
  • Green Lane Initiatives: Expedited customs clearance for verified sustainable goods is now active in 3 major ports.
  • Digital Documentation: Physical bill of lading requirements are being phased out in favor of blockchain-verified digital ledgers.
